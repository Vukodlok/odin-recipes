Pea and Cabbage Soup

Ingredients

  • 1 bag frozen green peas
  • 3 carrots chopped
  • 2 stalks celery chopped
  • 1 head green cabbage chopped
  • 4 cups chicken broth
  • 7 cups water
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/2 tablespoon onion powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Bring water and broth to a boil.
  2. Add seasonings.
  3. Add carrots and celery. Cook for 10-15 minutes, stirring.
  4. Turn heat to medium. Add cabbage. Cook for 15-20 minutes until cabbage is soft.
  5. Add bag of frozen peas. Turn off heat. Allow peas to warm through for 1-2 minutes.
  6. Serve. For added flavor combine with pierogi by added a few to the soup to act as dumplings.
